Shared platform with naming conflicts.

Context:
Multiple teams or projects share the same data platform instance, increasing the risk of object naming conflicts.

Implications for CI/CD:
- Resource names must be unique across teams and projects.
- Hardcoded or generic naming is not allowed.

Requirements:
- Enforce naming conventions using prefixes, suffixes, or namespaces (e.g., project, environment).
- Use schemas or logical isolation where supported.
- Parameterize all resource names in the pipeline.
- Ensure generated names are deterministic and consistent across environments.
