Different team owns orchestration.

Context:
The orchestration layer (e.g., schedulers, workflows, pipelines) is managed by a separate team and cannot be directly modified or deployed by this CI/CD pipeline.

Implications for CI/CD:
- Direct deployment or configuration of orchestration jobs is not allowed.
- Integration must happen through defined interfaces or handoff mechanisms.

Requirements:
- Generate deployment artifacts or configuration outputs that can be consumed by the orchestration team.
- Clearly define integration points (e.g., triggers, APIs, configuration files).
- Avoid including orchestration deployment steps in the pipeline.
- Document handoff steps and responsibilities between teams.
