Separation of duties required

Context:
Different roles must handle development, approval, and deployment to enforce governance.

Hard Constraints:
- MUST separate responsibilities between developers, approvers, and deployers.
- MUST NOT allow one user to control the entire deployment lifecycle.

Implications for CI/CD:
- Pipelines must enforce role boundaries.

Required Implementation:
- Enforce role-based access control (RBAC).
- Require approvals from users different than the code author.
- Restrict deployment permissions to specific roles.
