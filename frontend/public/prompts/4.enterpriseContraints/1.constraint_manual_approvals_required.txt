Manual approvals required

Context:
Certain environments (typically staging or production) require human approval before deployments can proceed.

Hard Constraints:
- MUST require manual approval before deploying to protected environments.
- MUST NOT allow fully automated deployment to production.
- MUST ensure approval actions are traceable.

Implications for CI/CD:
- Pipeline execution must pause for approval gates.
- Deployment flow must support human intervention.

Required Implementation:
- Implement approval steps in the pipeline for protected environments.
- Restrict approvals to authorized users or roles.
- Log all approval actions for audit purposes.
- Ensure rejected approvals stop the deployment.
